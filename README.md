# School Bell System

**Freeware**: for schools and classrooms.

A Windows desktop program that plays your school bell sounds automatically, at the exact times you schedule. Set it up once and leave it running — it handles the rest.

## How It Works

1. **Install** on any Windows PC or laptop in the school office or staff room.
2. **Set your schedule** — add time slots for each day of the week, assign a bell sound to each slot, and save.
3. **Connect** the computer's audio output to your school's PA or speaker system. Leave the program running.

The program checks the time every second and plays the correct sound automatically, with no further input needed.

---

## Features

- **Per-day schedules** — Monday through Sunday each have their own schedule, with as many time slots as you need.
- **11 sounds included** — Westminster chimes, classic bells, and intercom tones, ready to use out of the box.
- **Custom sounds** — load your own MP3 or WAV files if you prefer your school's traditional bell.
- **Next bell display** — a clear readout always shows which sound plays next and at what time.
- **Save & restore** — schedules are saved to a plain text file, easy to back up or copy to another machine.
- **No internet required** — runs entirely offline. No login, no account, no data leaves your computer.
